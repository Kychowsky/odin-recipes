Grilled CHeese

Description

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Cheese(Brie, Fontina, Gouda, Cheddar, Pepper Jack, Havarti)
  • Bread(Rye, Sourdough, White, Multigrain)
  • Butter

Steps

  2. Gather all Ingredients
  3. Prehead a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one slice of bread
  4. Place butter-side down on the hot skillet; add slice of cheese
  5. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  6. Cook until browned on one side;flip over and continue cooking until the cheese is melted
  7. Enjoy!
